Meet Steph

"Want to know more about me? Of course you do — and I wouldn't expect any different for your fur baby!"

My name is Steph. I live in South Liverpool and I am a pet lover through and through. I'm a mother of two and a grandmother, and I've had pet dogs all my life — so I truly understand the worries, concerns, and upset when leaving your best friend for whatever reason.

After the loss of my own beloved dog, I came to the decision that I couldn't experience that loss again. Therefore, I'm offering my care and companionship to pets where I can still have the joy of having these best friends in my life and caring for them whilst you complete your plans.

I am an outgoing, bubbly, and affectionate person, and I will be caring for your fur babies in my own comfortable, clean family home. I have a large, secure outdoor area to the back of my property for your pet, and there are walking areas and parks in close proximity to my home.

I also meet other dog walkers and owners when out on walks, so I can offer plenty of socialising for your pet. I am more than happy to discuss your pet and care requirements with you and willing to meet your requirements for your pet's needs.
